# Yasaflow Brand Guidelines

## Brand character

Yasaflow is:

- Modern
- Inclusive
- Calm
- Professional
- Reliable
- Simple
- Long-term

Yasaflow is not:

- Religious in its visual identity
- Childish
- Loud
- Cluttered
- Overdesigned
- Generic

## Audience

Yasaflow serves associations, volunteer organizations, sports clubs, churches, cultural organizations, mosques, student associations, faith communities and other membership-based organizations.

## Logo

The final official logo will be added later.

Until then:

- Use the reusable `Logo` component.
- Do not hardcode logo image paths throughout the site.
- The future logo must support horizontal, icon-only, light and dark variants.
- Preserve clear space around the logo.
- Do not stretch, recolor or distort the official logo.

## Visual signature

Use Yasaflow blue and teal carefully as recognizable accents. The visual identity should feel distinctly Yasaflow without copying another SaaS brand.
