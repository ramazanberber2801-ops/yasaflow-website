# Yasaflow Design System

## Principles

- Modern
- Calm
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Mobile-first
- Accessible
- Consistent

## Colors

- Primary blue: `#2185DC`
- Secondary teal: `#14B8A6`
- Dark surface: `#0F172A`
- Light background: `#F8FAFC`
- White card: `#FFFFFF`
- Main text: `#0F172A`
- Muted text: `#64748B`

Use gradients sparingly. The blue-to-teal signature may be used for selected brand accents, but never as visual clutter.

## Typography

- Use a clean modern sans-serif for website interface and body text.
- Use large, clear headings with strong hierarchy.
- Keep line lengths readable.

## Shape and spacing

- Rounded cards: generally 16–24 px.
- Buttons: generally 12–16 px radius.
- Use generous section spacing and whitespace.
- Prefer soft shadows and light borders over heavy effects.

## Components

- Primary button: blue background, high contrast, clear focus state.
- Secondary button: light surface with border.
- Cards: white or subtle tinted surface, clear title and short description.
- Inputs: visible labels, adequate touch targets and accessible focus styles.

## Responsive behavior

- Design mobile-first.
- Navigation must remain usable on small screens.
- Cards should stack cleanly.
- Do not rely on hover for essential actions.

## Icons

Use Lucide icons consistently. Do not mix icon libraries without an approved reason.
