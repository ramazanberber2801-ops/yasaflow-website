# Yasaflow Copy Guide

## Voice

Write in a way that is:

- Clear
- Calm
- Direct
- Helpful
- Professional
- Inclusive

Avoid hype, jargon and vague marketing language.

## Preferred wording

Norwegian product wording:

- `Opprett organisasjon`
- `Kom i gang`
- `Lagre endringer`
- `Inviter administrator`
- `Medlemmer`

English website wording:

- `Create Organization`
- `Get Started`
- `Book Demo`
- `Members`
- `Activities`

## Role terminology

- Owner: manages Yasaflow.
- Administrator: manages an organization.
- Member: belongs to an organization.

Do not use `members` when referring to administrators.

## Content rules

- No Lorem Ipsum.
- No invented customer testimonials.
- No fake statistics.
- No unsupported claims.
- Keep headings short and descriptive.
